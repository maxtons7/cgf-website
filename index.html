CGF Values Charter

The Causal Governance Framework (CGF) is founded on the principle that governance must serve the governed — not the gatekeepers. We reject systems driven by coincidence, inertia, and institutional apathy. Instead, we commit to intentionality, equity, and accountability in every dimension of our work.

  • Causality over Coincidence — Outcomes must be traceable to effort, policy, and intent — not to privilege, luck, or legacy.
  • Evidence-led Change — Every CGF position is informed by data, context, and the lived realities of those most affected.
  • Intergenerational Equity — Governance must serve tomorrow, not just today. We reject short-termism disguised as progress.
  • Global-Local Synergy — We act locally with an awareness of global structures — and push for coherence between the two.
  • Radical Inclusion — Marginalised voices are not invited as guests; they are positioned as architects.
  • Transparency with Teeth — We don’t just publish. We explain, simplify, and make space for challenge.

About Us

Who We Are

CGF was born out of necessity. It was forged through the lived experiences of ethnic minority leaders, veterans, UN diplomats, and policy strategists who recognised a common truth: the system is not broken — it was built this way. We are not a think tank. We are a strategic policy lab rooted in action, committed to dismantling structural inequality using data, diplomacy, and design.

Our Mission

To build a governance framework where outcomes are not accidental but engineered — where power is redistributed, and opportunity is no longer inherited, but made.
